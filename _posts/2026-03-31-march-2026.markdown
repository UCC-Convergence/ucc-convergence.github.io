Accountability is easy to demand, but far harder to practice. This March issue of *Convergence* examines what happens when responsibility is taken, and when it isn’t. From legendary Canadian astronaut Chris Hadfield’s reflections on risk and decision-making, to an exclusive interview with Aramark on the logistics of fuelling a campus, we peel back the curtain on the hidden systems that keep UCC and Canada running. We ask a simple question: who acts, and who answers for the consequences?
